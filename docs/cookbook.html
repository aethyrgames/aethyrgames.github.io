The spellbook.

Bite-sized incantations: one sentence you say, one thing Aethyr does. Skim for the move you need, say it in your own words, and let your AI pick the tools. Every recipe here lives inside the shipping build.

Most of these are reads: they can't change anything, so they're always safe to run. The author and refactor groups write, and every write goes through a compile gate and an automatic backup. Want the long-form stories instead? See the ritual walkthroughs.

⌖ Understand a Blueprint

Orient before you touch anything. Every recipe here is read-only.

⌖ “What does /Game/AI/BP_Enemy actually do? Give me the 10-second version.”

Aethyr returns a one-screen brief: parent class, variable and function counts, and the graph shape, without loading the whole asset. Use it to decide where to look next.

⌖ “Is the event graph on BP_Turret worth opening, or is it basically empty?”

Aethyr peeks at the graph and reports just its node count and a histogram of node kinds. You learn whether it is two nodes or two hundred before paying to read it.

peek_graph

⌖ “Pull the full picture on /Game/Items/BP_HealthPickup: parent, interfaces, variables, all of it.”

Aethyr reads the Blueprint metadata in one pass: parent class, implemented interfaces, every variable, and summaries of each function and graph. This is the full orientation when the 10-second brief is not enough.

⌖ “Show me how the event graph on BP_Enemy is actually wired up.”

Aethyr returns the real node-by-node topology of the event graph: every node, its pins, and the connections between them. This is the wiring, not a summary.

⌖ “Read me the ApplyDamage function on BP_Turret, signature and body.”
